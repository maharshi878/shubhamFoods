Garlic:

Procedure:

-Sourced Locally
-Sun Dried
-Tray Dryer
-Bulb Breaker Machine, to obtain seperated Cloves
-Cloves are peeled using peeler machine
-Cloves are washed and dried in centrifugal drying machine
-Colour Sorted to eliminate rotten or spoiled cloves
-Manually sorted further
-Packed and sold, formed further as follows:

--Peeled Frozen garlic

--Freeze dried garlic and banana, to be expanded further.

--Vacuum Fried Garlic, and other products like Okra, Potatoes, Chickpeas, Kidney Beans, Sweet Potatoes, Banana.

--Roasted Masala Garlic

--Black Garlic
